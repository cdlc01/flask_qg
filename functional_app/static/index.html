Hate Speech Predictor


Welcome To The Hate Speech Predictor Application

This app predicts whether a given tweet is hate speech or not (you can leave the hashtags and mentions in there, it looks at those too)

More specifically, this hate speech predictor is especially designed to work with hate tweets within the past few years (according to the FBI and multiple groups, hate crimes and speech have been on the rise since approximately 2014). This application will continue to grow and evolve as more hate speech (and non hate speech is fed into it)
